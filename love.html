Forever My Valentine

In your eyes, I found my home, A gentle warmth I’ve always known.
Your smile turns my fears to light, My darkest days feel soft and bright.
Your laughter heals my quiet pain, Like sunshine after falling rain.
With every breath, my heart does say, “I choose you more with every day.”
Through every storm and peaceful shore, I’ll love you deeper, more and more.
My soul is yours, my heart, my mind— In you, my forever love I find.
So on this day of hearts and signs, I give you all of me as mine
Not just today, but all my life— Forever yours, my Valentine.
